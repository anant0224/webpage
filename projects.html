The following are the relevant course projects I have completed

  • Furniture Design from Pose

    Guide: Prof. Siddhartha Chaudhari
      Modeled furniture design as an optimization problem such that for a given pose of a human being, the region of contact between the body and the surface is maximized, with additional terms for smoothness and collision prevention.

  • Optical Sorter

    Guide: Prof. Kavi Arya
      Developed an embedded system consisting of a Raspberry Pi board with mounted camera and indicative LEDs to automatically detect defective items in a manufacturing plant.

  • Poisson Image Editing

    Guide: Prof. Ajit Rajwade
      Used interpolation machinery based on solving Poisson equations to perform tasks in seamless cloning (concealment, insertion, feature exchange) and selection editing (texture flattening, local color changes).

  • PDE based Image Regularization

    Guide: Prof. Suyash Awate
      Implemented a PDE based image regularization algorithm and demonstrated applications like in-painting, JPEG block artifact removal, noise reduction and flow visualization.
